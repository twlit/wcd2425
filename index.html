About New Horizons Academy

At New Horizons Academy we strive to support and empower students who are hardworking and driven towards success. All of our students recieve industry related teaching by using a hands-on approach. We believe that this allows our students to be best prepared for the working environment. Additionally, we push our students to take advantage of all opportunites local employers who need workers give them in order to further grow their skills.
